Our Story

Built by chemists who got tired of vague purity claims.

BioCode Peptides is a peptide design, synthesis, and quality-verification company headquartered in Cambridge, Massachusetts. We exist to bring pharmaceutical-grade analytical discipline to a research supply category that has historically lacked it.

Origin

From a shared bench to a dedicated facility

BioCode Peptides began in 2013, when a small group of peptide chemists — several with backgrounds in solid-phase synthesis and analytical chromatography from contract research work around the Kendall Square biotech corridor — set out to build a supplier they themselves would trust.

The founding thesis was simple: a research peptide is only as useful as the confidence a scientist can place in its identity and purity. Rather than compete on price or catalog size alone, the company invested early in in-house analytical capability — HPLC and mass spectrometry — so that every claim on a label could be checked against data, not just a supplier's word.

That principle has guided every stage of growth since, from the earliest shared lab space to today's Cambridge headquarters and network of cGMP-aligned manufacturing partners.

Mission

To give researchers peptides they can trust — backed by documented specifications, independent verification, and full transparency about manufacturing and testing.

Vision

A research supply chain where purity and identity claims are always verifiable, never just asserted.

Milestones

Company timeline

2013

Founded in Cambridge, Massachusetts

BioCode Peptides is established by a founding team of peptide chemists, initially operating out of shared laboratory space within the Kendall Square life-sciences district.

2015

In-house analytical laboratory established

Preparative HPLC and analytical QC capability brought in-house, enabling lot-by-lot purity verification ahead of release.

2017

Manufacturing partnership network expanded

Formal quality agreements established with cGMP-aligned contract manufacturing partners in the United States to scale production while preserving analytical oversight.

2019

Certificate-of-Analysis program formalized

Every released lot begins shipping with a lot-specific Certificate of Analysis covering identity (MS) and purity (HPLC) data.

2021

Scientific Advisory Board convened

An external advisory board is formed to periodically review synthesis methodology, specification rigor, and testing protocols.

Facilities

Cambridge headquarters & manufacturing network

Our headquarters at 100 Cambridge Research Parkway houses R&D, analytical QC, quality/regulatory, and customer operations. Production is carried out through a network of U.S.-based, cGMP-aligned contract manufacturing partners, each operating under a formal quality agreement with BioCode Peptides.

This model lets us combine dedicated analytical oversight with manufacturing scale — every partner facility is subject to our incoming and outgoing QC protocols regardless of where synthesis physically occurs.
