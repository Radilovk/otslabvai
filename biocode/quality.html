Quality System

What we test, how we test it, and what we don't claim.

This page is our most detailed statement of fact: manufacturing standards, the release-testing protocol every lot goes through, current certification status, and the regulatory limitations of our products.

Regulatory Status — Please Read

BioCode Peptides manufactures and sells research peptides for laboratory and research use only. Our products are not approved by the FDA as drugs, biologics, dietary supplements, or medical devices, and are not evaluated for safety or efficacy in humans or animals. Nothing on this website should be read as a therapeutic, diagnostic, or dosing claim. Statements about manufacturing standards (e.g. "cGMP-aligned") describe our internal quality processes and those of our manufacturing partners — they are not a claim of FDA drug-manufacturing approval.

Manufacturing Standards

Facility & process controls

cGMP-Aligned Production

Manufacturing partners operate under process controls aligned with current Good Manufacturing Practice principles: documented batch records, equipment qualification, and change control.

Controlled Raw Materials

Amino acid derivatives and resins are sourced from qualified suppliers with incoming certificate-of-analysis review prior to use in synthesis.

Segregated Lot Handling

Each production lot is tracked independently from synthesis through packaging, preventing cross-contamination between sequences.
