Who We Are

A science-first peptide company, built by chemists.

BioCode Peptides was founded in Cambridge, Massachusetts — inside one of the world's densest biotechnology corridors — by a small team of peptide chemists and analytical scientists who were frustrated by inconsistent purity claims across the research supply industry.

Today we operate as a peptide design and quality-verification company: every sequence we release is synthesized against a documented specification, purified by preparative HPLC, and confirmed by mass spectrometry before it ever reaches a lab bench.

Our Story & Leadership

Our operating principles

  • Specification first. Every peptide ships against a published identity and purity spec, not a marketing claim.
  • Independent verification. Purity and identity are confirmed by third-party laboratories, not self-reported alone.
  • Full disclosure. Certificates of Analysis are tied to lot numbers and made available on request.
  • Research use, stated plainly. Our products are intended for laboratory and research applications — we say so clearly, everywhere.
The Science

Built on analytical chemistry, not adjectives.

Peptide quality is measurable. We treat it that way — from solid-phase synthesis through final release testing.

Solid-Phase Synthesis

Sequences are built residue-by-residue using Fmoc solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS), the same methodology used across academic and pharmaceutical peptide chemistry.

Preparative HPLC Purification

Crude peptide is purified via reverse-phase preparative HPLC to isolate the target sequence from truncation and deletion byproducts.

Mass Spectrometry Confirmation

Final identity is confirmed by MS, with purity independently cross-checked against analytical HPLC traces on every released lot.
